EPC SOLUTIONS PROVIDER

WIZTEQ, an Engineering, Procurement and Construction Company, has been leading the change in providing Green Energy Solutions to extensive and diverse array of industry areas globally. Well equipped with infrastructure, vast experienced team, rich industry knowledge and execution expertise. We have undertaken and accomplished turnkey projects and variety of integrated comprehensive Design to Delivery solutions to diverse industry sectors. As a turnkey project supplier, we are single point solutions provider for the entire spectrum of Plant Design & Build; right from evaluation, assessment, feasibility study, equipment manufacturing, procurement of critical bought outs to complete installation, including commissioning, operation & maintenance assistance. Here, flexibility is one benefit that we can deliver as our technical team is capable of developing a generalized engineering concept for working out unusual designs and in carrying engineering knowledge from one field to another. We also support our valued customers for equipment selection, optimizing costs, alternate supplier supports with proven products, plant output enhancement, revamping, relocation of plants, etc.
